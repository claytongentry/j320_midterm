Mudd Library Closes

Students grapple with new library environments

By Clayton Gentry

On March 25, the Seeley G. Mudd Library at Northwestern University closed its doors to facilitate a major renovation and expansion project. Construction began on December 1, 2015, and is scheduled to be completed by August 1, 2017.
